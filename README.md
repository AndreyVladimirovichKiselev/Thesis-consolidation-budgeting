# Consolidated Reporting & Group Budgeting Model

Excel-based model to prepare consolidated financial statements and a 5‑year top‑down budget for a group with different ownership levels.

## Goal
Provide a unified view of the group’s results without distortions from intercompany transactions; perform plan‑vs‑actual and factor analysis of net profit; deliver a budget and key takeaways for management.

## Solution
- Full consolidation for subsidiaries B and C, equity method for subsidiary D.
- Intercompany eliminations with transparent logic on a dedicated sheet.
- Non‑controlling interest calculated and correctly reflected in P&L and balance sheet.
- Factor analysis of net profit variance by margin, volume, structure, and intercompany effects.
- 5‑year top‑down budget (2026–2030) with planned intercompany flows.

## Results
- Clean consolidated reports without intercompany distortions.
- Quantified contribution of each subsidiary to the net profit variance.
- Working 5‑year budget model that can be recalculated with new inputs.
- Management presentation (5–7 slides) with key metrics and assumptions.
